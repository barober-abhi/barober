Barober: Refund Policy

Understanding our refund process for services and bookings

Key Aspects of Our Refund Policy

Eligible Scenarios

Conditions under which you are eligible to receive a refund for your booking.

Non-Refundable Cases

Circumstances where refunds will not be provided, ensuring clarity.

Processing Time

Details about how long it takes to process a refund once it has been confirmed.

1. Eligible Refund Scenarios

You may be eligible for a refund in the following cases:

  • Order Cancelled by the User (More Than 2 Hours Before Service Time): A full refund will be issued with no cancellation charges.
  • Order Cancelled by the User (Within 2 Hours of Service Time): 80% of the booking amount will be refunded. A 20% cancellation fee is charged.
  • No Barber Accepted the Order Within 20 Minutes: The system will automatically cancel the order, and no charge will be applied.
  • Barber Fails to Show Up or is Late by More Than 30 Minutes: If the assigned barber does not arrive within 30 minutes after the scheduled service time and the user chooses to cancel the booking, a full refund will be provided.
  • Service Not Provided Due to Technical Error: If your booking is affected due to a system issue or failed payment, and no service is rendered, you will receive a full refund.

2. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • The service has already started or is marked as completed.
  • The user provides incorrect address or is unavailable at the service location.
  • Attempted misuse of the cancellation system.

3. Refund Processing Time

Refunds for eligible cancellations will be processed to the original payment method within 1 day of cancellation confirmation. The actual credit to your bank or wallet may depend on your payment provider.
